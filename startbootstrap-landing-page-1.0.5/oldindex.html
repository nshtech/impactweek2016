About NSH Impact Week

Prize donations
1st place: $1,000
2nd place: $500
3rd place: $350
4th place: $200

NSH Impact Week is an annual competition hosted by Northwestern Student Holdings which provides money to and raises awareness about causes supported by NU's student organizations. Four student-run organizations have each partnered with an NSH company to make fun and informative music videos. The NU community is invited to vote on these videos from Sunday, May 10th to Friday, May 15th. All four philanthropies will be awarded monetary prizes, with specific prize amounts determined by the final vote count.


Schedule

Opening Event
6:30-7:30PM, Sunday, May 10
Annenberg G21

Impact Week Kickoff Dinner will gather our four teams of student groups and NSH companies to present their music videos and launch voting!

Voting, at the tent or online
9AM-5PM and all day, May 11 through 15
The Arch and Facebook

Stop by the Impact Voting Tent at the Arch where your vote for your favorite group counts double and you'll get a free raffle ticket, candy, and balloons!


Plus, Vote on Facebook through a like for your favorite group and help them win the $1000 prize!

Festival
2-5PM, Friday, May 15
Norris East Lawn

Come to our Impact Festival where nine participating NU service groups are hosting hands-on service projects that need your help. For every project you participate in, you get a raffle ticket for great prizes, like free credit for BoxCo. storage!

About NSH

NSH is comprised of four different student-run companies that serve Northwestern and the greater community: BoxCo., NU|Tutors, DesignWorks, and RezEssentials. These companies employ more than 50 Northwestern students and had a combined revenue exceeding $400,000 last year. NSH members get real-world entrepreneurial experience, learn key business skills and work in exciting and fast-paced environments.
