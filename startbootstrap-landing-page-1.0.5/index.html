About Impact Week

NSH Impact Week is a week full of community events hosted annually by Northwestern Student Holdings. NSH is partnering with four philanthropic organizations on campus to raise money and awareness for their causes. These four organizations will compete throughout the week by hosting events and campaigning on the Sheridan for votes. The NU community is invited to vote in support of a cause each day of impact week (May 8 - May 13, 2016). The philanthropy with the most votes will be awarded the grand prize donation. The other philanthropies will also receive donations of an amount corresponding to the voting results.
